  1. Shakespeare Shit - 2021/09/29 @approx. 1:00PM

    "What a piece of work is man?"/"Man delights not me." - Act 2 Scene 2 Line 301
    Hamlet is explaining how dull and non-existant the world (life) feels to him. For example, saying how air, "this majestical roof fretted with golden fire" appears to be a nothing "but a foul and pestilent congregation of vapor".
